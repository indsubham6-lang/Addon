# Linkumori Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 23, 2026

Linkumori is a free, non-commercial, open-source tool.

## Information We Collect

Nothing.

Linkumori does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

All extension data is stored locally on your device. No information is sent to any external servers. When you uninstall the extension, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

## Remote Rules (Optional Feature)

The Remote Rules feature is disabled by default.

When enabled, you may choose to provide custom URLs from which the extension can download additional URL-cleaning rules.

### When Remote Rules Are Enabled

- The extension downloads rule files from the URLs you specify.
- Your browser connects directly to those third-party servers.
- Those servers may collect standard web server information (such as IP address, browser type, request logs, etc.).
- Linkumori does not control, monitor, or store any data from these connections.
- We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party servers. Please review their privacy policies before enabling this feature.
- This feature is intended exclusively for advanced users who have full control over both their physical and virtual infrastructure (for example, servers that host rules or software that manages server operations).

### Your Responsibility When Using Remote Rules

Linkumori is a tool. When you enable the Remote Rules feature and provide a custom URL, the extension simply executes a fetch request as directed by you.

We do not determine, influence, or control what URL you add, what server you point to, or what data is served. Those decisions are entirely yours.

By providing a URL, you accept that:

- It is **your responsibility** to ensure the URL you provide complies with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction, including but not limited to data protection and privacy laws.
- It is **your responsibility** to ensure you have lawful authority over the physical and virtual infrastructure behind that URL.
- Linkumori bears no responsibility for the content, legality, or consequences of URLs you choose to add.

Since this feature is intended exclusively for advanced users who maintain full control over both their physical and virtual infrastructure, you are the **Data Controller** for any data processing that occurs through that connection. You determine the purpose (why you choose to use this feature and what you intend to achieve by connecting to your infrastructure) and the means (how data is collected, handled, and processed within your infrastructure). Because you control both the physical layer (hardware, servers, and network equipment) and the virtual layer (software, operating systems, services, and configurations), every decision about that connection is yours alone. You may choose to log connections, restrict them, disable them entirely, or configure them in any way you see fit. That is entirely your choice and entirely your responsibility. Linkumori is simply the instrument through which your instructions are carried out; responsibility for lawful use rests entirely with you.

Linkumori is a tool that operates strictly on your instructions: it fetches only what you direct it to fetch, nothing more and nothing less.

### When Remote Rules Are Disabled

Linkumori operates entirely offline using only its built-in rules. No external network requests are made.

## Withdrawal of Consent and Data Deletion

You may withdraw your consent at any time simply by uninstalling the extension or add-on. Upon uninstallation, all data stored locally by Linkumori on your device is permanently and automatically deleted. No further action is required on your part.

Since Linkumori does not transmit any data to external servers, there is no remote data to delete.

## Age and Legal Consent
